Toggle Aperture to see why signals get weaker with distance: your antenna captures a fixed area of the expanding shell. At 10m, it's a meaningful fraction. At 50m, the shell is 25× larger but your antenna hasn't grown — same bucket, vastly more ocean. The green wireframe sphere shows the total shell; the green disc at the RX is what your antenna actually catches. 2× distance = −6 dB (¼ power).
