# Lesson 12: Vertical Antennas — Ground Planes and Monopoles

**Unit:** 3 — Design & Construction  
**Prerequisites:** Lesson 11  
**Equipment needed:** Wire/rod, SO-239 connector, VNA  
**Estimated time:** Theory 25 min + Lab 60 min

---

## Learning Objectives

- Explain monopole/image theory (half a dipole + ground reflection)
- Design a quarter-wave ground plane antenna
- Understand the role of ground radials and droop angle
- Build and VNA-validate a ground plane vertical
