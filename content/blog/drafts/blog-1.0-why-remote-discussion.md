## The Four Points (Summary)

1. **Feedpoint Location** — Why 100m when 20m would work? Learning, 
   experimentation, and engineering reasons (counterpoise, noise).

2. **RF Environment** — Modern houses generate HF noise via conducted
   paths. Granite walls block radiated noise but not conducted. Distance
   is the real solution.

3. **Antenna Orientation** — Null at house (Config A) vs broadside at
   house (Config B). Valley alignment. Blog 2.0 rotation experiment.

4. **Counterpoise** — The strongest argument for remote deployment.
   Near the house, counterpoise = everything. Remote = controlled system.
