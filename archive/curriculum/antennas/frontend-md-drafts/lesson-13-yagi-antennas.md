# Lesson 13: Yagi-Uda Antennas — Adding Gain

**Unit:** 3 — Design & Construction  
**Prerequisites:** Lessons 11–12  
**Equipment needed:** Modelling software, construction materials, VNA  
**Estimated time:** Theory 30 min + Lab 90 min

---

## Learning Objectives

- Explain how parasitic elements create directivity
- Define the roles of reflector, driven element, and directors
- Understand the gain-bandwidth trade-off in Yagi design
- Model a Yagi in 4nec2 and build/validate with VNA
