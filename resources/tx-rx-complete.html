Stage 1 — Electrons Flow: The transmitter drives an alternating current through the dipole. Electrons oscillate back and forth — they don't travel from end to end, they slosh. Current (green) is maximum at the feed point and zero at the tips. Voltage (red) is the opposite — maximum at the tips where charges pile up.
